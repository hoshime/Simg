ABOUT US

We Make Your Filming Possible

The next generation of film making in Nepal starts from here. It begins with recognition of the immense potential held by Nepal for shooting. The diversity of Nepal, within its confines, carves exotic and great shooting locales. We excel at scouting perfect locations for our clients. Since our inception, we have been working with domestic as well as international production houses with immense mutual satisfaction. Our service is multi-disciplinary in both its approach and execution which enables us to work in both fictional and non-fictional contents.

Our scope of work is diverse. From television Documentaries that are related to current affairs- incorporating human interest stories, as well as Wildlife and Diversity of Asia, our spectrum of vision has made us a trustworthy partner. Our active network of journalists, professionals and researchers enable us to provide all types of media support and solutions. International journalists, photographers or film makers we are committed to provide unrelenting service to all. Our extremely competent multi-lingual fixers, efficient production managers and an experienced team of professionals make us the most sought after filming service provider in Nepal.
